FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about working with Nitro Wolf.

How much does a website cost?

Pricing varies based on project scope, complexity, and timeline. A basic informational website typically starts at ₹50,000. E-commerce platforms and custom web applications are priced higher. We provide a detailed quote after understanding your requirements.

How long does a typical project take?

A landing page typically takes 1–2 weeks. A full website takes 4–8 weeks. Complex e-commerce or web application projects can take 2–4 months. Timelines depend on how quickly feedback and content are provided by the client.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?

Yes! We offer monthly maintenance packages that include updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and content updates. Packages start at ₹5,000/month. Ask us about our retainer options.

What information do you need to start?

To kickstart a project, we need a clear brief (your goals, target audience, competitors), any brand assets (logo, color palette, fonts), content or a content plan, and your timeline and budget expectations.

Do you work with clients outside India?

Absolutely. We work with clients globally. All communication is handled via email, video calls, and project management tools. Payments can be made in INR or USD.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes, website redesign is one of our specialties. We conduct a full UX audit of your existing site, identify areas for improvement, and redesign with both aesthetics and performance in mind — while preserving your SEO equity.

What technologies do you use?

We work with a modern stack including React, Next.js, WordPress, Shopify, Webflow, and custom HTML/CSS/JS. The technology choice depends on your project's specific needs and long-term goals.

How do revisions work?

Each project includes 2–3 rounds of revisions per phase as stated in the project agreement. Revisions are best provided as consolidated feedback in one document. Additional revision rounds beyond scope are billed at ₹2,500/hour.

Still Have Questions?

Reach out directly and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
